Development and Design

I am a Front End engineer. I believe that design and development go hand in hand - and that a well executed user experience should be approached in a way that gives complex information, clarity. My passion is in using all the tools at my displosal to accomplish this simple goal.

I leverage my experiences in technology, art, and creative problem solving to bring a fresh perspective and combination of skill to every project.
