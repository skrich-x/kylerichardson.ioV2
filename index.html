Development and Design

I am a Front End engineer. I believe that design and development go hand in hand - and that a well executed user experience should be approached in a way that gives complex information clarity.

I crave a challenge and push myself to learn constantly. As a Front-End Engineering Graduate of The Iron Yard Academy I gained experience in many modern web development technologies and best practices. Before begining my career in code I worked as a computer technician, spending my days troubleshooting and correcting hardware, network, and security issues in several modern operating systems.

My passion lies in using all the tools at my disposal; leveraging my experiences in technology, art, and creative problem solving to bring a fresh perspective and unique combination of skills to every project - creating thoughtful and intuitive experiences.
